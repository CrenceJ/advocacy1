About Guidance

Guidance facilitates people throughout their lives to manage their own educational, training, occupational, personal, social, and life choices so that they reach their full potential and contribute to the development of a better society. Guidance Counselor helps students in academic achievement and mental health concerns. In June 2007, Mrs. Corazon B. Kawi, RGC, RP, RPm was appointed as the third Director. In November 2012, the In 2010, the Guidance Center opened its (so-called) satellite office at the Maryheights Campus, Bakakeng, to serve the students of the School of Accountancy and Business Management. In 2013, the second satellite office was put up at the Navy Base Campus to serve the students of the School of Teacher Education.



Objective

  1. To assist students:
    • In gaining clearer understanding of themselves and their world;
    • In their satisfactory adjustment to various situations.
  2. To provide opportunities via learning experiences that will help answer the educational needs of the students for them to become more responsible in their studies.
  3. To assist the administration in implementing school policies especially with respect to its vision for the students.
  4. To provide the following Guidance Services:
    • Counseling/ Guidance Service
    • Information Service
    • Testing/ Appraisal Service
    • Scholarship and Financial Assistance Programs
    • Extension Services
    • Career/Educational and Follow-up Services
